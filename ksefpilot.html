Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

KSeF Pilot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application for managing Polish tax invoices with KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur) integration. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you log in to the app, we process:

  • Username and password (sent directly to your Odoo server, not stored on our servers)
  • Session token (stored locally on your device for auto-login)

1.2 Invoice Data

The app processes invoice data including:

  • Seller and buyer information (company name, NIP, address)
  • Invoice details (amounts, line items, payment terms)
  • KSeF submission status

All invoice data is stored on your organization's Odoo server. The app does not store invoice data on external servers.

1.3 Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes on invoices and capture document photos
  • Microphone: For voice input functionality
  • Storage/Photos: To save and select document images
  • Location: For business address verification (optional)
  • Notifications: To send local reminders about invoice status

These permissions are requested only when needed and can be denied without affecting core functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with your organization's Odoo server
  • To display and manage your invoices
  • To submit invoices to the KSeF system
  • To look up buyer information via GUS/VIES registries

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Login credentials are transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Session tokens are stored locally using secure storage on your device
  • All business data resides on your organization's Odoo server
  • The app does not have its own backend server — it connects directly to your Odoo instance
  • No data is shared with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the following services as part of its functionality:

  • Your Odoo Server: For all business data operations
  • KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur): Polish government e-invoice system, via your Odoo server
  • GUS/VIES: Public business registries for buyer NIP lookup, via your Odoo server

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Retention

  • Local session data is cleared when you log out
  • Invoice data retention is managed by your organization's Odoo server administrator

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Log out at any time to clear your local session
  • Revoke device permissions through your phone's Settings
  • Request data deletion through your organization's Odoo administrator

7. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
